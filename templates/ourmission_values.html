EMPOWERING YOUNG PEOPLE, BUILDING A STRONGER FUTURE – OUR MISSION, VISION, AND VALUES

UDAAN Society is driven by a clear mission: ensuring all young people and children have access to the support they need, whenever they need it. Whether it’s financial aid, education, or simply a reassuring conversation, we strive to provide a helping hand and a path towards a brighter tomorrow.

Our vision is a world where every child and youth feels empowered and supported. We envision a world built on equality, justice, and peace, where everyone has the opportunity to thrive. Our dedicated team embodies these values – service, responsibility, determination, and kindness – as we work to break down barriers and create a more inclusive society.

Explore our website to learn more about our mission, vision, and the impactful programs we offer. Let’s build a brighter future, together.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to make sure all young people and children get all the support they need, when they need it, no matter what it is.

Our Mission is to create an infrastructure of easily accessible services for people, mainly for the underprivileged society, employing labour and materials available in active participation-ready communities, resulting in an inclusive society.

Whether it is a reassuring conversation, financial help, education, professional training, or simply the assurance that they are not alone, we will make sure that everyone gets support that meets them where they’re at as quickly as possible.

OUR VISION

We want to build a world where no child or youth feels alone and gets every possible help they need as soon as possible.

To make people’s life easy by applying the humanistic approach based on equality, justice, peace and bring them on the way of sustainable development by involving them in main stream of society.

Our entire team wants to see a world where every youngster struggling in any way feels able to reach out and has people who’ll help them with education, jobs, and more. We want to spread out in every region so we’ll be reachable to all.

OUR VALUES

  1. Service: The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.
  2. Responsibility: You can’t escape the responsibility of tomorrow by evading it today.
  3. Determination: A dream doesn’t become reality through magic; it takes sweat, determination and hard work.
  4. Happiness: Love is when the other person’s happiness is more important than your own.
  5. Kindness: Love is when the other person’s happiness is more important than your own.
  6. Contribution: The human contribution is the essential ingredient. It is only in the giving of oneself to others that we truly live.
