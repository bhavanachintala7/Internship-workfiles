Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about UDAAN Society and how you can support our mission.

What does UDAAN stand for? +
UDAAN stands for Unified Development Association for Amelioration of Neglected.
When was UDAAN Society registered? +
UDAAN Society was registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860 on 13 December 2004.
Where is the headquarters of UDAAN Society? +
The head office of UDAAN Society is located in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India.
In which states does UDAAN Society work? +
Currently UDAAN works across several districts in Uttar Pradesh including Aligarh, Bulandshahr, Firozabad, Banda and Mahoba, and previously worked in Rajasthan, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh and NCR region.
What are the main focus areas of UDAAN Society? +
The organization focuses on women and children development, water and sanitation, livelihood, environment protection, health awareness, and community training programs.
How can I contribute to UDAAN Society? +
You can support UDAAN Society by donating through the website donation page or by contributing to their official fundraising campaigns.
Do donors get tax exemption? +
Yes, donations made to UDAAN Society are eligible for 80G tax exemption certificates.
How can I join or volunteer with UDAAN Society? +
You can join as a volunteer, intern, or fellow by applying through the career and fellowship section on the official website.
How can I contact UDAAN Society? +
You can contact the organization through the contact form on the website, email, or the official office located in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh.
